Our Story

Born in the verdant valleys of Wayanad, Pupae carries the wisdom of nature and the warmth of human connection. We believe that every child deserves products created with intention—products that honor both the delicate skin of infants and the precious earth that sustains us.

Our name, Pupae, symbolizes transformation and growth. Like the gentle metamorphosis of a butterfly, we guide your child through every stage of their journey with products that feel like an embrace from nature itself.

Every thread, every fiber, every detail reflects our commitment: to create a legacy of care that extends from the crib to the community, from your home to the green hills of our homeland. Pupae isn't just a brand—it's a promise of purity, sustainability, and love.
